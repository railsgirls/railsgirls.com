2nd Rails Girls Workshop in Frankfurt, 7-8th March 2014

iGude! We are happy to annouce the 2nd Rails Girls Workshop in Frankfurt on 7-8th March 2014.

You will discover the world of web development in a fun and relaxed atmosphere, together with about 40 other participants. Within a single day, you'll learn how to build your own little dynamic web application.

The event is free and a team of about 15 coaches is looking forward to help you code and will answer all your questions.

The event is adressed to women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part. For more details, see the FAQ below.

Applications are closed now.
We will send out confirmation mails within the next days.

FAQ

How much does the participation at Rails Girls workshops cost? Nothing, it's free! You just need to be excited!

Who is this aimed for? Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part. Most of the speeches are given in english, but small group work is done in german. Please bring your laptop.

Can men attend? Yes, but you need to be accompanied by an interested lady. Also, girls are given a priority.

I know how to program - How can I help? We’re also looking for people to be coaches. We’ll have a two-three hour workshop before the event to walk you through the curriculum. Email us
