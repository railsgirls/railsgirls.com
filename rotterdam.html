Friday 18th January

20.00 - 22:00

Installation party

Come get Rails installed by our bunch of pro’s, along with a funky looking terminal, easy-to-use texteditor and more. There’ll be pizza. And drinks. And musical relief, as Frank Goverts will perform some of his songs while we get things set up.

You’ll find Mangrove on the top floor of the Schieblock-building near Central Station (Schiekade 189, Rotterdam).

Sponsors, boyfriends, girlfriends and developers that happen-to-be-in-the-neighborhood are more than welcome to join.
