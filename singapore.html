Rails Girls Singapore 28 June 2014

Did you know that the first international version of Rails Girls happened in Singapore in 2011?

It's been a while, but we are going to do it again on 28 June 2014 (Sat), the day after RedDotRubyConf 2014!

Join the one-day crash-course and learn how to build web applications using Ruby on Rails, with a Singaporean twist.

To register, head over to our Meetup group now.

Tweet

You will learn basic Ruby on Rails by doing it with help from our friendly coaches.

You just need your own laptop, curiosity and a dose of imagination.

FAQ

Who is this for?
Women with basic knowledge of working with a pc. We’ve had people of all ages taking part.

Can men attend?
Men are welcomed too, but you need to be accompanied by an interested lady.

Also, ladies are given priority.

What to bring?
Please bring along a working laptop and power.

I know how to program - How can I help?
We’re also looking for people to be coaches. Email us!
