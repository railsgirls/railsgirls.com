Rails Girls Charlotte July 26th-27th 2013

Hello world!

Rails Girls comes to Charlotte! During the free two-day workshop we'll dive into the magical world of Ruby on Rails.

Applications are now closed.

You learn designing, prototyping and coding with the help from our coaches.

Schedule:
Friday, July 26th, 2013

6 pm - 8 pm

Installation Party

Get know your coaches and peers. Bring your laptop so we can help you install Ruby on Rails using the install guide.

8 pm - 10pm

Coaches Dinner

After the attendees clear out we will eat some food and go over The Plan™ for the big day. You won't want to miss this!

Schedule: Saturday, July 27th, 2013

9 am - 10 am

Registration

Presentations

  • Sponsors
  • Presentations

Workshop

Lunch

FAQ

How much does the workshop cost? Nothing, it's free! You just need to be excited!

Who is this aimed for? Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part. Don't forget your laptop!

Can men attend? Yes, but you need to be accompanied by an interested lady. Also, girls are given a priority.

I know how to program - How can I help? We’re also looking for people to be coaches. We’ll have a two-three hour workshop before the event to walk you through the curriculum. Email us
