Rails Girls Washington, DC

The next workshop will be June 13, 2013! We're pairing up with RubyNation on this one.

Come to learn or teach about building web applications with Ruby on Rails. Here in DC, we're about more than just politics and monuments, we have a strong technology community who loves Ruby and Rails.

In the meantime, we are doing meetups. Sign up!

Follow @RailsGirlsDC on Twitter for the latest news!

Meetup Information

The meetups are casual, drop-in evenings for anyone to come and work on rails. The meetup will go from 5-9pm. We’ll do short introductions at 6:30. Feel free to bring your own project to work on, or we’ll also have a few options/suggestions and some coaches around to help out if we get stuck. *These meetups are open to anyone who wants to attend... you don't have to be a RailsGirls alum*

FAQ

How much does the meetup cost? Nothing, it's free! You just need to be excited!

Who is this aimed for? Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part, most of the crowd usually is women and men in their twenties or thirties, but we truly welcome everyone.

Can men attend? Yes, but you have to help women learn!

I know how to program - How can I help? We’re also looking for people to be coaches. You are welcome to come and help!
