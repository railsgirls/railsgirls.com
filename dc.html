Rails Girls DC
1-2 June 2016
GWU Law School, Arlington, VA

Here in DC, we're about more than just politics and monuments, we have a strong technology community that loves Ruby and Rails. Rails Girls DC hosts 2-day workshops to introduce women to Ruby, Rails and the local programming community.

We're happy to announce that we're back and bigger than ever with our annual Rails Girls DC Workshop and our first ever Rails Girls Next Unconference!

Our workshop will once again be run alongside RubyNation.

Rails Girls Workshop

Join us for our sixth Rails Girls DC event: a free 1.5-day workshop where participants will discover the exciting world of web development through Ruby on Rails. With the help and guidance of our coaches, attendees will create a real web application in a fun and relaxed atmosphere while meeting new and experienced developers. You can find out more about Rails Girls here.

Rails Girls Next

Rails Girls Next is a day-long unconference that provides women the chance to talk about the topics they care about most in a comfortable setting. Participants decide on the discussion topics at the beginning of the day together. Topics will be both technical and non-technical and will be discussed in small groups (no more than ~30 women in a group). All levels are welcome and you will also have an opportunity to meet with Rails Girls Workshop participants. More information on unconferences can be found here.

You don't have to be a Rails Girls workshop alum to attend. If you are looking to get into developing for the first time, we encourage you to apply to the Rails Girls DC Workshop.
