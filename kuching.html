Rails Girls Kuching September 9 & 10 2016

Hello world!

Rails Girls comes to Kuching for the third time! Join the two-day crash-course on the exciting world of building web applications with Ruby on Rails.

Ruby on Rails is a framework that you can build web apps on. Some apps that are built on Ruby on Rails are Groupon and Says.com.

Applications will be opened by 13th July 2016

No previous coding experience needed!

Anyone of any age and gender can apply. Limited seats! Females and beginners will be prioritized.

FAQ

How much does the workshop cost? Nothing, it's free! You just need to be excited!

Who is this aimed at? Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part. The event and talks will be conducted in English. Please bring your laptop.

Can men attend? Yes, but women and girls will be given priority for admission.

Can I bring a Mac/Windows/Linux laptop? Yes, a laptop of any operating system works. You just need to have a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, IE 9+) installed.

Is there a dresscode? Nope, just wear whatever that's comfortable for you.

I know how to program - How can I help? We’re also looking for people to be coaches. We’ll have a two-three hour workshop before the event to walk you through the curriculum. Sign up or email us .
