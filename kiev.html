Rails Girls Kyiv June 21-22 2014, Ukraine

Ласкаво просимо!

We are very sad to announce that RailsGirls at June in Kiev 2014 has to be cancelled.
Lot of things happening in Ukraine now. Many other conferences in Ukraine (PyCon, iOSonRailsConf and Euruko, just to name a few) were canceled this year. We hope you understand the circumstances under which we have taken this decision.
We will organile workshop as soon as the situation in Ukraine gets more stable.

Cheers,
Kiev, RG team.
