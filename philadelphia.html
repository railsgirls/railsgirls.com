Rails Girls Philadelphia, PA 15-16th March 2013

Hello world!

Rails Girls comes to Philadelphia! During the one and a half-day workshop we'll dive into the magical world of Ruby on Rails.

Tweet Follow @railsgirlsphl

Saturday 16th March

Let's rock and code!

This is a fun, hands-on workshop where you will learn the following:

  • Understanding the difference between dynamic and static web pages
  • Basic components of a web application
  • ABC's of coding including strings, methods, variables, arrays, blocks... as well as the tools (terminal, browser, text editor).
  • Concepts including Rails Generators, Scaffolds, Gem Management, REST/Resources, Controllers, Views, etc.
  • Basics of creating an app

FAQ

How much does the workshop cost? The workshop will cost $10.

Who is this aimed for? Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part. Please bring your laptop.

Can men attend? Yes, but you need to be accompanied by an interested lady. Also, girls are given a priority.

I know how to program - How can I help? We’re also looking for people to be coaches. We’ll have a two-three hour workshop before the event to walk you through the curriculum on March 5th. Email us if you are interested in coaching! Read more about being a coach here
