FAQ

How much does it cost? Nothing, it's free!
You just need to be excited! And you need to show up ready to learn.
If you need to cancel Contact Us. There are limited spots.

What will I learn?
You'll learn designing, prototyping and coding with the help from our friendly coaches.

Who is this for?
The workshop is aimed
at beginners to programming. Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer are encouraged to attend.

If you already program and are curious about Rails feel free to go through the tutorial on your own. It is laid out step by step.

Can men attend?
Women are given priority, but men are welcome as long as they are accompanied by an interested woman.

Can men Coach?
We love cool male coaches. We want to have a mix of male and female coaches.

You do I need? You need your own laptop, curiosity and a sprinkle of imagination!

How can I help?
We can always use your donations for future events.
