Rails Girls Galway 20-21st June 2014

Hi Girls :)
Let's learn together how to make new things with the Web!

Rails Girls comes to Galway again! During the *free* two-days workshop in June 2014, we will dive into the magical world of Ruby on Rails and build an application together, from scratch.

Applications are now opened!
Places are limited so be sure to submit your interest

Applications close: June 5th
Acceptances informed: June 8th

Location: INSIGHT Centre for Data Analytics (ex DERI building)
IDA Business Park, Lower Dangan
Galway City, Ireland

You learn designing, prototyping and coding with the help from our coaches.

Workshop Program

18pm - 20pm

Installation Party

  • Bring your laptop so we can help you install Ruby on Rails and everything else you'll need using the INSTALL GUIDE.
  • If there is no way you can make it on Saturday and need help with installation make arrangements to come at 9am on Saturday. We want to keep exceptions to the bare minimum Please be respectful of our volunteer coaches' time.
    Where: Insight Centre for Data Analytics (ex DERI building), first floor

Schedule: Saturday, June 21, 2014 9 am - 6 pm

9 am - 10 am

Registration

Register, find your seat, get your name tag and meet some people.

Late Installations

Presentations
