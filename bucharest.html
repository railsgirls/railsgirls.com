Rails Girls Bucharest May 30-31, 2014

Rails Girls is a free workshop for girls and women who want to get a glimpse of web development in a friendly atmosphere.

Tweet

You learn designing, prototyping and coding with the help of our coaches.

You need your own laptop, curiosity and a sprinkle of imagination!

Follow @RailsGirlsBuc on Twitter or watch our Facebook page for the latest news!

FAQ

How much does the workshop cost? Nothing, it's free! You just need to be excited!

Who is this aimed for? Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer and good command of English. Most of the materials are in English, the talks will be given in Romanian. Please bring your laptop.
