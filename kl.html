Rails Girls KL

Hello world!

Ever wondered what a programmer does at work? Ever wanted to build the next Facebook?

This free event is for beginner women who want to learn more about coding and building things. We will learn about the web, prototyping, basic programming and get introduced to the world of technology.

Ruby on Rails is a framework that you can build web apps on. Some apps that are built on Ruby on Rails are KFIT and Touristly.

Apply now!

This workshop is suitable for beginners to coding.

For those who couldn't make it the last event, please do come for this one. No prior experience required, you just need to bring your curiosity.

Previous participants are invited to join our advance class that will happen at the same time. We will explore deeper rails concepts in the advance class to finally build our web app.

FAQ

How much does the workshop cost? Nothing, it's free! You just need to be excited!

Who is this aimed for? Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. We’ve had people of all ages taking part. The event and talks will be conducted in English. Please bring your laptop.

Can men attend? Yes, but women and girls will be given priority for admission.

Can I bring a Mac/Windows/Linux laptop? Yes, a laptop of any operating system works. You just need to have a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, IE 9+) installed.

Is there a dresscode? Nope, just wear whatever that's comfortable for you.

I know how to program - How can I help? We’re also looking for people to be coaches. We’ll have a two-three hour workshop before the event to walk you through the curriculum. Sign up or email us email us .
