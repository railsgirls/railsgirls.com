FAQ

Is this going to be in English? Nope, most of the event and talks are going to be in Spanish! The local coaches are native speakers. However, some of the inspirational talks as well as instructions might be in English.

How much does the workshop cost? Nothing, it's free! One more reason to attend!

Who is this event for? Women of any age with basic knowledge of working with a computer. Most of the program is run in Spanish. Please bring your laptop to fully experience the event.

Can men attend? Yes you can, but you need to be accompanied by a lady attendee. Also, girls are given a priority in our waiting list.

I know how to code - How can I help? We’re also looking for coaches. We’ll have a two-three hour workshop before the event to walk you through the curriculum. Register as Rails coach!
